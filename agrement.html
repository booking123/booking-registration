The purpose of this Channel Partner Agreement (the "Agreement") dated as of the Effective Date set forth in the signature block below, is to summarize the terms and conditions of the services to be performed by and between BookingPal, Inc. a Delaware corporation (“BookingPal”), with offices located 101 Pacifica, Suite 250 Irvine, CA 92618, and you as (“Channel Partner

  1. BookingPal Obligations
    1. BookingPal shall make information about lodging units (“Rental” or “Rentals”) available to Channel Partner. information about such Rentals (referred to herein as “BookingPal Content”) including but not limited to; photos, property description, Rental rates, availability calendar, cancellation policies, check-in instructions and other terms and conditions associated with each Rental. The exact amount of available inventory of Rentals and related BookingPal Content shall be determined by BookingPal in its sole discretion.
    2. BookingPal shall provide a web service for Channel Partner to query BookingPal Content. Other web services may include: a payment interface and booking engine ("Services")
    3. BookingPal will ensure that it has contracts in place with Rental owners and managers supplying Rentals so that reservations booked hereunder will be honored.
  2. Channel Partner Obligations
    1. Channel Partner will use BookingPal’s web services to display BookingPal Content or Services in connection with Channel Partner’s Website(s) and/or Mobile Apps as listed herein. Channel Partner’s use of the Services shall be limited to display BookingPal Content on the Website(s) or Channel Partner Mobile Applications. Channel Partner will not publish or distribute the BookingPal Content in any other form or through any other media without prior notification to BookingPal. Channel Partner shall not add, remove or modify BookingPal Content.
    2. Channel Partner agrees not to make any representations, warranties or other statements concerning BookingPal, BookingPal's site, any of BookingPal's products or Services, or BookingPal's site policies, except as expressly authorized by BookingPal.
    3. Channel Partner agrees not to create or purchase URLs or keywords that include the specific names or allude to specific names of BookingPal, Inc.
    4. Upon the execution of this Agreement both Parties shall work together to complete the integration of BookingPal Content into the Channel Partners Website(s) and/or Mobile Apps as listed herein.
  3. Payment Terms
    1. Where Rental Owner/Manager is the Merchant of Record, BookingPal will pay Channel Partner Fifteen (15) days after the end of each calendar month for payments due and collected. Where Channel Partner is the Merchant of Record, Channel Partner will be responsible for paying within the Terms of each Rental Owner/Manager, and BookingPal within Fifteen (15) days after the date of BookingPal’s invoice. Where Channel Partner is the Merchant of Record and has not accepted the payment Terms of the Rental Owner/Manager, Channel Partner will be responsible for paying Rental Owner/Manager, and BookingPal within Fifteen (15) days after the date of BookingPal’s invoice.
    2. Where a payment is required hereunder, the Parties hereto agree to (I) make such payment on time as required herein, and (ii) at the time of such payment, remit a brief statement or report containing information (i.e. gross Rentals, deposits, credit card discounts, any fees, etc.) necessary to fully understand the nature of the actions and transactions for the amount received. The Parties to this Agreement agree that they will provide fully transparent information relating to the usage of the BookingPal Content and full analysis thereof upon the request of the other Party.
